About Senniah Gowder & Co

Profile

The business deals with promoting products based on coir, ropes, and jute from Senniah Gowder & Co.

Senniah Gowder & Co was founded before 1950s on Rangai Gowder Street (Big Bazaar/Townhall), which is a historic business market place in Coimbatore. The shop was opened offering rope and essential products for cows from around the city for customers to take home.

With decades of experience in manufacturing and supplying quality products, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in Coimbatore and across Tamil Nadu. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice for businesses and individuals alike.
